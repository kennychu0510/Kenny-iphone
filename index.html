About me:

I am currently a student of the MicroMaster in A.I. & Programming bootcamp course from the Tecky Academy in cohort 20. I obtained my bachelor's degree in civil engineering in 2019 and worked in the civil engineering field as a graduate for 2 years before realizing that field is not right for me. My first encounter with programming was when I was studying a compulsory programming course in university as an engineering student. The language taught in that course was C++ and I enjoyed it a lot. Unfortunately, I did not pursue for the computer science major at that time due to various reasons.

I decided to quit my job in 2020 November and I started researching for different paths to help me land a job in the programming field. After seeking advice from friends, I decided to enrol in the Tecky Academy bootcamp course. Before the course started, I spent my free time doing self-preparation by going through some free courses such as https://www.freecodecamp.org/ and Harvard's CS50 series.

My current aim is to finish the bootcamp course and step into the field of software engineering.
